My Projects

Electric wheelchair

I designed a wheelchair that can be controlled by bust inclination. Under the seat are four force sensors which send information to an Arduino. This Arduino then processes the data and controls the speed and orientation of the wheels using a program of my own design.

Electric skateboard

I designed a skateboard that can be controlled by bust inclinaison. Under the board are two force sensors which send information to an Arduino. This Arduino then processes the data and controls the speed and orientation of the wheels using a program I designed myself. The computer program is less complex than for the wheelchair, but installing the sensors was a difficult step.

International Mobility

As a student at ENSEEIHT, my international mobility experience has been a transformative chapter in both my academic and personal life. I had the privilege of spending a semester at Dublin City University (DCU) in Dublin, Ireland, which offered me a unique opportunity to expand my horizons and immerse myself in a different educational and cultural environment. At DCU, I was able to explore cutting-edge courses aligned with my field of study, benefiting from a new perspective on engineering and technology while collaborating with students and faculty from diverse backgrounds. The experience challenged me to adapt to a different academic system, embrace new learning methods, and enhance my problem-solving skills in dynamic and multicultural settings. Beyond academics, living in Dublin allowed me to experience Irish culture firsthand, from its warm and welcoming people to its vibrant traditions and breathtaking landscapes. This international mobility not only strengthened my technical expertise but also fostered invaluable personal growth, such as greater independence, improved intercultural communication, and a broadened worldview. It reinforced my belief in the importance of global collaboration and equipped me with skills that will undoubtedly be assets in my future career. Overall, my semester at DCU was a defining moment that enriched my education and prepared me to thrive in an increasingly interconnected world.
