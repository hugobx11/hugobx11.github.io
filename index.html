My Projects

Electric wheelchair

I designed a wheelchair that can be controlled by bust inclination. Under the seat are four force sensors which send information to an Arduino. This Arduino then processes the data and controls the speed and orientation of the wheels using a program of my own design.

Electric skateboard

I designed a skateboard that can be controlled by bust inclinaison. Under the board are two force sensors which send information to an Arduino. This Arduino then processes the data and controls the speed and orientation of the wheels using a program I designed myself. The computer program is less complex than for the wheelchair, but installing the sensors was a difficult step.
